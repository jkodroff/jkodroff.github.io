About Me

0001-01-01

My name is Josh Kodroff and I’m a technolgy craftsman living in Philadelphia. Craftsmanship, to me, means 3 things: An increased emphasis on quality in order to deliver the best long-term value to stakeholders. Empathy for my teammates, users, and clients. A commitment to continuously improving my skills, both technical and interpersonal. I spent the first 12 years of my career in software development with an emphasis on message-driven architectures, domain-driven design, continuous integration/delivery, and agile (small “a”) tools and techniques.
