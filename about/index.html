About Me

My name is Josh Kodroff and I’m a technolgy craftsman living in Philadelphia.

I’ve spent most of my career developing enterprise software, and since I have to go to work every day, most of my learning has been focused on making that as painless as possible. Some things that I’ve learned about and applied include DevOps, domain-driven design, and message-based architectures like CQRS.

When I’m not working or doing code stuff I enjoy cooking, making music, and hiking in the Wissahickon.
