About

Josh Kodroff is a technologist living in Philadelphia.  He works as a Sr Cloud Consultant at 2nd Watch, a professional services and managed cloud provider with organizations in all stages of cloud adoption, from startups to large enterprises, with a focus on software delivery and application modernization.

Josh strives to express his dedication to craftsmanship in 3 ways:

  1. An emphasis on quality and integrity in order to deliver the best long-term value to stakeholders.
  2. Empathy for his teammates, users, and clients.
  3. A commitment to continuously improving his skills, both technical and interpersonal.

Josh’s focus since 2016 has been in cloud-native computing, DevOps, and modern IT operations.  Before moving toward operations, he spent 12 in software development with an emphasis on message-centric architectures, domain-driven design, and continuous integration/continuous delivery.

Outside of work, Josh enjoys cooking, meditation, playing music, collecting records, and hiking in the Wissahickon.
